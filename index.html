SafeForHumansAI.com: Developing AI Systems to Preserve Privacy and Safety

My name is Mark Watson. I have been working in the field of AI since 1982. While I have always hoped that my work would in general benefit sopciety, recent advances in AI, primarily in large language modles like GPT-3, have made me realize that we need to be very careful about how we use AI. I am now working on AI systems that preserve privacy and safety.

You can find my books on my personal web site https://markwatson.com. I am the author of 20+ books on Artificial Intelligence. I have 55 US Patents.
